Our Story

The Honey Pot was founded in March 2019 with the mission to solve a challenging aspect of those with food allergies: baking. From recipe testing in our kitchen, to a full blown bakery in Vancouver, we're happy to say we suceeded in numerous desserts free from the most common allergens.
